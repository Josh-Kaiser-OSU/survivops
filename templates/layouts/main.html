  • Home Page - allows the customer to view all products (products table).
  • Sign In Page - allows the customer to sign in or sign up (customers table).
  • Product Page - allows the customer to access information for a single product (products table).
  • Cart Page - allows the customer to access the carts associated with them (carts table).
  • Order Page - allows the customer to place an order (customers, orders table).
  • Account Page - allows the customer to view order history and update account info (customers, orders tables).
  • Contact Page - allows the customer to get contact info and see the FAQs.
  • Admin Page - allows the administrator to view all customers, view a customer's orders, and manage products (customers, orders, products).
